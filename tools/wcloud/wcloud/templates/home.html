This is the WebLab-Deusto creation wizard. With it, you can automatically generate your own WebLab-Deusto system, on which you can manage your users, grant them permissions on certain laboratories, etc.

The process is very simple: click on the Configure tab and fill the data related to your institution (university, secondary school, etc.). Then, click on the Deploy tab to fill the administrator data and deploy the system. Once deployed, you will be able to modify users (including the administrator data) and permissions, but not the parameters provided in the Configure panel. You may however change those by contacting us.

Enjoy!
